Install a Wireless NIC

A wireless NIC enables a computer to connect to a wireless (Wi-Fi) network. Wireless NICs use PCI or PCIe expansion slots on the motherboard. Many wireless NICs are connected using a USB connector.

To install a wireless NIC, follow these steps:

Step 1. (Figure 1) Find an empty PCI slot on the case and remove the small metal cover.

Step 2. Align the card to the appropriate expansion slot on the motherboard.

Step 3. (Figure 2) Press down gently on the card until the card is fully seated.

Step 4. (Figure 3) Secure the cardâ€™s mounting bracket to the case with the appropriate screw.

Installation Tip: Some cases have small slots at the bottom of the hole where the cover was removed. Slide the bottom of the mounting bracket into this slot before seating the card.
