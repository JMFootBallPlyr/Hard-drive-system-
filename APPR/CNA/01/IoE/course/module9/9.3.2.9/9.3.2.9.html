Speakers

Before replacing laptop speakers, check that the volume is not muted by increasing volume or unmuting the sound.

To remove and replace the speaker unit, follow these steps:

Step 1: Power off the computer and then disconnect the AC adapter.

Step 2: Remove the battery and any other components recommended by the manufacturer, including the keyboard or top casing, as shown in the figure.

Step 3: Disconnect any cables connecting the laptop to the motherboard.

Step 4: Remove any screws securing the speakers to the laptop frame.

Step 5: Remove the speakers.

Step 6: Insert the new speakers.

Step 7: Tighten all screws to secure the speakers to the laptop frame.

Step 8: Connect any cables connecting the laptop to the motherboard.

Step 9: Insert the battery, along with all other components that were removed.

Step 10: Connect the AC adapter and power on the computer to check for functionality.
