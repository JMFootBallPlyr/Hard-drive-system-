Install the Optical Drive

Optical drives are installed in 5.25 inch (13.34 cm.) drive bays that are accessed from the front of the case. The bays allow access to the media without opening the case.

To install an optical drive, follow these steps:

Step 1. Position the optical drive so that it aligns with the 5.25 inch (13.34 cm.) drive bay opening at the front of the case, as shown in the figure.

Step 2. Insert the optical drive into the drive bay so that the optical drive screw holes align with the screw holes in the case.

Step 3. Secure the optical drive to the case using the proper screws.

Installation Tip: Slightly hand-tighten all the screws before tightening any of them with a screw driver. This will make it easier to tighten the last two screws.
