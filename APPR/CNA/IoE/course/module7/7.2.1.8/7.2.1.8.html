Comparing the OSI and TCP/IP Models

The OSI model and the TCP/IP model are both reference models used to describe the data communication process. The TCP/IP model is used specifically for the TCP/IP suite of protocols, and the OSI model is used for the development of communication standards for equipment and applications from different vendors.
